What is LegitMMO?

LegitMMO is a top-down MMO set in a living urban world. Build your story from the ground up—whether you’re a CEO, rogue hacker, graffiti artist, or underground racer. Every choice leaves a mark. Trade, create, hustle, and rise. This is your city.

Key Features

Living World

The city adapts to player decisions. Every event and action leaves a lasting impact.

Player Economy

From street food to skyscrapers—everything is made, sold, or stolen by players.

Gear-Based Roles

There are no fixed classes. Define yourself by your gear, your skills, and your choices.
